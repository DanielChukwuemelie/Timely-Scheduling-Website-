    AN INTRODUCTION TO TIMELY

    How Timely Works

    STEP ONE

    Create a Scheduling Link

    Connect your calendar and Timely will help you create scheduling links and booking pages based on your availability. Share your Timely link with others so people can book time with you in one click.

    STEP TWO

    Team-based scheduling

    Booking links can be based on different event types - from 15-minute Zoom calls to 30-minute in-person meetings – and rules. Include other members of your team, route the meeting to the right people on your team, and collaborate around scheduling requirements.

    STEP THREE

    Workflows and integrations

    Workflows and integrations connect Timely to your process and other tools. Send reminders, follow-ups, and connect your CRM, video conferencing, and even billing platforms!
